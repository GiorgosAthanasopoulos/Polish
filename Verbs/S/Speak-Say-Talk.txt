I speak/say/talk = Ja mówię (moovie)
You speak/say/talk = Ty mówisz (moovish)
He/She/It speaks/says/talks = On/Ona/Ono mówi (moovi)
We speak/say/talk = My 
You speak/say/talk = Wy
They speak/say/talk = Oni/One 

Example:
    - Ja mówię po polsku   (I speak polish)
    - Ja mówię po angielsku (I speak english)
